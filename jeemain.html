WELCOME TO BMTS,

Must Know Tips to Score High in

JEE Main

  NTA conducted JEE Main 2020 online papers from

9th to 12th January. For all the JEE Main aspirants,

now is the second chance to buckle themselves up to

face fierce competition in the race to achieve their

goal. The second session for JEE Main 2020 is in April.

There is no harm in attempting the exam second time

as this is the time to improve your score, also if the

candidate appears in both January and April sessions,

the better of the two scores will be considered.

Now, studying and practising for JEE Main in a wellplanned

and disciplined manner can only give you

a good result and help you in achieving success. If

you practice well in this limited time, then you can

definitely make it through JEE Main 2020.

If you are willing to give your 100% , here are the

must-know-JEE Main-Preparation- Tips, that will ease

your struggle and help you work in the right direction.

Formulate a Realistic Study Plan

It is the most important step for the preparation. Firstly

you should be well aware of the exam pattern and

the type of questions asked and the important topics

that use to ask in the exam. Know the weightage of

each chapter and important topics for JEE Main exam

from the graphical analysis given in this book.

Based on your understanding, formulate a Study Plan,

by which you should be able to cover all important

topics from each subject.

Also, it is advisable to study for all three subjects

together, rather than taking one subject at a day or for

some days. Moreover, now you are not left with much

time to take your favourite subject first then come to

the other subjects. Another important point is to

read both class 11 and 12 topics simultaneously. Your

study plan should be well-distributive. Remember to

spend your Time like Money.

After first session of JEE Main 2020, as you will be

left with almost 2 months for the second session.

In this short time, the best thing is that you must do

the practice wisely.

Practice, Practice and More Practice

For that, solve as much as Previous Years’ Papers

and Mock Test Papers. For online practice visit

www.bmts.ml It is an excellent tool to assess

your preparedness with detailed report. You will get

to know on which sections or topics you need to put

in more efforts and how.

Do Self Evaluation

You are your best judge. Only you can push yourself

to outperform. Solve Previous Years’ Papers and

Mock Test Papers every day and see where you need

improvement.

These are some important tips, which we hope

will help you in your JEE Main preparation in a

much easier and fruitful direction. You should

keep yourself updated with the JEE news and

announcements.

Difficult roads often lead to beautiful destinations.

Believe in yourself. You can do it!

All the best!!
